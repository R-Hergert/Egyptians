Apep

Apep, or Apophis is the demon of chaos, and the ultimate foe of Ra. He was said to have represented all that was outside of the ordered world. In other words he was responsible for all the chaos that occurs in the world. Although there were many different forms of snakes, which had certain meanings like royalty, or divinity, Apophis did not represent any of these qualities. It was said that he would encounter Ra every night, and attempt to stop him from passing through the underworld. Ra would try and pass through the underworld on his divine bark, where he would need to defeat Apophis in order for the sun to rise again. The god Seth rode as a guardian on the front of the bark, where he slay Apophis every night. It was believed throughout egypt that the king could aide Ra on his journey by performing rituals against Apophis.1
