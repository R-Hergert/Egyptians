Kebechet

Kebechet is the goddess of freshness she is the daughter of Anubis and his consort Anput. Kebechet is thought to help her father Anubis in his role of the god of embalming, Kebechet is pictured as a snake and sometimes with a body of stars but other sources state that she is depicted as a women with the head of a snake.
