Nu

Nu has no gender which he is at all time. It has two different forms which it can take with Nu being the male version, and Nunet being the female version. He is thought to have been the “waters of chaos”. It is said that Nun was the first god, and that four gods lived inside of his waters of chaos, four frog gods who were male, and four snake goddesses who were female. They together formed the first land mass. Nu is portrayed as a frog headed man with blue skin, or a snake headed woman with blue skin. He is sometimes depicted as holding a solar bark, similar to Ra’s. There is no known temple which is dedicated to Nu, nor is there any known cult centre either.1
